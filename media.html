Are Financial Advisors Prepared for Digital Assets?

  • by jeni,
  • may 31, 2024
  • in Are Financial Advisors Prepared for Digital Assets?
5.k views

Individuals worldwide began accumulating bitcoin shortly after the mining of the genesis block in 2009. More recently, institutional investors have also started to explore the world of cryptocurrencies. But what about financial advisors and wealth managers? Are they prepared to embrace digital assets and recommend this new asset class to their clients? Judging by the enthusiasm at last week’s National LINC 2020, TD Ameritrade's annual conference for financial advisors, the answer appears to be a resounding "yes." Over 1,000 financial advisors gathered at the Hyatt Regency Orlando for a day-long Digital Assets track. The event was hosted by Don Friedman, founder of the Digital Asset Strategy Summit, and Ric Edelman, RIADAC founder and one of the nation's leading financial advisors. Discussions focused on the impact of blockchain on the global economy and the potential role of digital assets in client portfolios.
