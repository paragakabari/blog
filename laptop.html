By implementing this program, the Gujarat State Government aims to enhance educational opportunities and support the academic growth of tribal students in the state.

Primary Goal:
Provide new computers to children who identify as indigenous, native, or tribal. Promote academic success among tribal students in Gujarat.

Target beneficiaries:
Impoverished children across Gujarat. Children of workers registered with the Gujarat Labour Welfare Board.

Program Benefits:
Free computers for students from financially disadvantaged backgrounds. Financial assistance to support students in completing their online coursework.

Objectives:
Encourage and motivate students to pursue their passions and aspirations. Provide financial support through a loan scheme.

Loan Scheme Details:
Financial support in the form of a loan under the Laptop Sahay Yojana 2024. Annual interest rate of only 6%. Loan repayable in up to sixty monthly payments. A 2.5% penalty in addition to the current interest rate if the loan amount is not repaid on time.
