WHAT IS HOME LOAN EMI CALCULATOR

205 k views

The Home Loan EMI Calculator assists in computing the Equated Monthly Installment (EMI) for your home loan. This tool aids in planning your finances for purchasing or constructing a new house by estimating the monthly EMI amount you'll need to pay.

The Loan EMI Calculator also provides you with the Total Interest payable over the entire loan tenure. The Total Amount you pay to settle your loan equals the Loan Amount plus the Total Interest accrued.

HOME LOAN EMI CALCULATION METHOD
✿ Calculating home loan EMI in Excel is simple using the PMT function.

✿ The PMT function requires three parameters: Interest Rate, Remaining Months, and Principal Value Remaining.

✿ The interest rate provided should be the monthly home loan interest rate divided by 100.

✿ Remaining months refers to the number of months left for which EMIs are yet to be paid.

✿ Principal value is the amount of outstanding principal remaining in your loan account.

✿ Remember, your EMI comprises two components: the Principal Amount and the Interest Amount.

✿ The Interest Amount is higher initially and decreases over time.

✿ The Principal Amount is lower initially and increases over time.

Elements of Loan EMI

✿ Fundamentally, a loan EMI consists of two components: the Principal Amount and the Interest Amount.

✿ The principal amount starts low initially and increases over time.

✿ The interest amount is higher initially and decreases over time.

✿ The majority of the interest amount is paid during the first half of your loan tenure.

✿ As you approach the closure of your loan by paying all EMIs on time, there is very little interest left to be paid, making it challenging to save on interest using the loan prepayment option.

✿ Therefore, it is advisable to make loan prepayments during the initial half of the loan term if you aim to maximize interest savings.

Factors influencing Loan EMI

✿ Two factors impact Loan EMI: Interest Rate and Tenure.

✿ The higher the interest rate, the more interest amount you will need to pay.

✿ The longer the tenure, the greater the amount of interest you will end up paying.

✿ To save the maximum amount of interest, aim to secure a loan with the lowest possible interest rate and shortest tenure.

✿ Schedule a call to learn more about loan EMI calculations and strategies for saving on loan interest.

Strategies for Saving on Loan Interest

✿ As mentioned earlier, loan interest can be reduced by opting for loans with lower interest rates, selecting shorter tenures, or making prepayments on existing loans.

✿ When making loan prepayments, remember to shorten the tenure to maximize interest savings on your home loan.

✿ Reducing the loan tenure will save more on interest compared to reducing the EMI amount.

Common Errors to Avoid

✿ To maximize the benefits of your Home Loan EMI Calculator and EMI planning, steer clear of these common mistakes:

✿ Avoid underestimating your EMI: Ensure your EMI is manageable within your budget to prevent financial stress.

✿ Don't overlook interest rates: Carefully assess them as they have a significant impact on your EMI and total loan cost.

✿ Overlooking Tenure Considerations: A shorter tenure results in higher EMIs but lower interest payments, whereas a longer tenure lowers the EMI but increases the total interest cost. Finding the right balance is crucial.

✿ Lack of Sufficient Research: Failing to compare loan options from different lenders can result in missed opportunities for better terms.

✿ Neglecting Extra Payments: Failing to take advantage of the option to make additional payments can prolong the loan tenure and increase interest payments.
