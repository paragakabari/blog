What is an e-Challan?

An e-challan is a digital fine issued by the traffic police for traffic violations. You can check an e-challan online to view details such as the fine amount, the current status of the challan, and an option to pay it electronically. e-Challans can be accessed through the Parivahan portal or the Spinny tool. A straightforward, step-by-step guide is available for paying your e-challan online, allowing you to resolve the fine conveniently without needing to visit the court.

How does the e-challan system work in India?

With rapid digitalisation, the e-challan system is able to work automatically as it is directly linked to the central database. Any traffic violations caught on traffic cameras will automatically generate the respective e-challan. Once issued, a notification is sent to the car owner of the traffic violation and the pending fine. The car owner can use the link provided in the notification to view details of the challan and pay the fine online.

Advantages of the e-Challan System

The traffic e-challan system offers several advantages for both drivers and traffic police.

Convenience:
The e-challan system allows vehicle owners to conveniently check and pay their traffic challans online. This removes the need to visit the police station or court to pay the fine.
Transparency:
Violations and fines are updated in real-time, ensuring accuracy and eliminating concerns about errors or manipulation.
User-Friendly:
The online e-challan system simplifies tracking pending traffic fines for vehicle owners and offers multiple payment options for convenient settlement of e-challans.
How to Check Your Traffic Challan Status Online

Enter your car’s registration number and click "Submit" to view any issued e-challans and check the status for your vehicle. All pending and unpaid e-challans will be displayed, along with an option to pay the fines and clear the challan. Checking your e-challan online provides a convenient way to stay informed about your car's challan status and allows you to pay the fines with minimal hassle.

How to Pay Your Traffic e-Challan Online

Once you check your e-challan status online on Spinny and see pending fines, you can conveniently and securely pay them online. All e-challan payments must be made through the Parivahan platform. Paying your e-challan through Parivahan allows you to avoid a court visit and facilitates the quick sale of your car by clearing any outstanding liabilities.

Documents Needed to Avoid Traffic Challans in India

When driving, it’s crucial to carry essential documents to avoid additional traffic fines. The documents you need to keep on hand include:

Driving license

Registration Certification (RC)

Valid insurance

Pollution under Control (PUC) certificate

Pay e-challan Using Parivahan

Follow these steps -

Step 1:
Go to the Parivahan website
Step 2:
Click on e-challan System
Step 3:
You will be redirected to a new page. Click on 'Get Challan Details'
Step 4:
Enter the Registration number of your vehicle, followed by last 5 digits of the chassis number or engine number. Complete the captcha and click on 'Get Detail'
Step 5:
The details of your e-challan will be shown. If the information is accurate, click the Pay Now button
Step 6:
Select your preferred payment option to make the payment
