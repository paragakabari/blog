The RTO (Regional Transport Office) is a government agency responsible for various transport-related matters within a specific region or jurisdiction.
The Regional Transport Office (RTO) is a pivotal division under the Ministry of Road Transport and Highways (MoRTH), tasked with administering and regulating transportation within its designated region. RTOs operate branches across districts or states, forming a crucial network for managing vehicle ownership and driving regulations. These offices are central to activities such as issuing licenses, registering vehicles, enforcing traffic laws, and certifying vehicle fitness.

The functions of the RTO include administering vehicle registration, issuing driving licenses, enforcing traffic regulations, and conducting vehicle fitness certifications.:


✿ Vehicle Registration: The RTO plays a crucial role in licensing and registering the entire fleet of vehicles within its jurisdiction. This involves issuing Registration Certificates (RCs), maintaining a record of vehicle plates, and ensuring that all registered vehicles receive their plates.

✿ Issuance of Driving Licenses: The RTO is responsible for overseeing all aspects related to driving on public roads, including conducting driving tests and issuing licenses to qualified individuals.



How to Check Vehicle Ownership Details Online



1. Accessing the VAHAN Portal:

✿ There are two primary ways to access the VAHAN portal:

✿ To access the VAHAN portal, visit the official Parivahan website (https://parivahan.gov.in/). Navigate to the "Informational Services" section and select "Know Your Vehicle Details." This will direct you to the VAHAN portal.

✿ Alternatively, you can directly access the VAHAN portal by visiting https://parivahan.gov.in/.

✿ Providing vehicle information:

Once you've accessed the VAHAN portal, you will encounter a form that asks for the following information:

✿ Vehicle Registration Number: This refers to the unique alphanumeric code displayed on the vehicle's registration plate.

✿ Captcha Code: Enter the displayed captcha code to verify that you're not a bot.

✿ Viewing ownership details:

✿ If the information provided is valid, the portal will show details about the vehicle, such as the owner's name, registration date, vehicle class, and engine specifications.
