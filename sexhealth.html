The Evolution of Nutritional Science

Nutritional science has transitioned from a narrow focus on fundamental nutrients—such as proteins, fats, carbohydrates, vitamins, and minerals—to a broader exploration of bioactive compounds found in foods. These bioactives encompass fibers, phytonutrients, bioactive fats, and ferments.
